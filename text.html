The options can be combined. Add zero-width marks inserts non-printing, zero-width characters (specifically the Unicode U+200E LEFT-TO-RIGHT MARK and U+200F RIGHT-TO-LEFT MARK characters) into the text. These are invisible, although some editors/applications may purge them. With the Prepend every word mode, only the LTR mark is used before each word. Use special spaces replaces the spaces with U+2005 FOUR-PER-EM SPACE. Replace dashes replaces U+002D HYPHEN-MINUS with U+2010 HYPHEN and U+2014 EM DASH with U+2015 HORIZONTAL BAR. Add footnote on copy is different: instead of hidden characters, it appends a visible plain-text disclosure line to the end of the copied text.
